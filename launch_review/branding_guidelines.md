# KaizenOS Branding Guidelines

For logo, imagery, and visual asset creation.

---

## Brand Essence

### Name Origin
**Kaizen** (改善) - Japanese philosophy meaning "continuous improvement" or "change for better"
**OS** - Operating System for your productivity/life

### Brand Personality
- **Intentional**: Every feature serves a purpose
- **Calm**: Not overwhelming or gamified
- **Honest**: Shows reality, not vanity metrics
- **Progressive**: Small improvements compound over time

### Tone of Voice
- Direct and clear
- Confident but not aggressive
- Helpful but not hand-holding
- Professional but human

---

## Visual Direction

### Logo Concepts

**Concept 1: Continuous Loop**
A circular or infinite loop symbol representing continuous improvement. Could incorporate a subtle calendar grid or progress arrow.

**Concept 2: Ascending Path**
Steps or incremental rises suggesting progress over time. Minimal, geometric.

**Concept 3: Connected Dots**
Abstract representation of connecting goals to actions to calendar events. Network/constellation style.

**Concept 4: K + Circle**
Monogram using "K" integrated with a circular element (sun, compass, or cycle).

### Logo Requirements
- Works at small sizes (favicon, app icon)
- Recognizable in monochrome
- Clean, minimal aesthetic
- No gradients (flat design)

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

**Deep Navy** - `#1a1f36`
- Primary text, headers
- Conveys focus and professionalism

**Soft Blue** - `#4f46e5` (Indigo-600)
- Primary actions, links
- Represents clarity and trust

**Clean White** - `#ffffff`
- Backgrounds, cards
- Creates breathing room

## Typography

### Font Stack
- **Primary**: Inter or system-ui
- **Monospace**: JetBrains Mono (for code/technical elements)

### Hierarchy
- **H1**: 2rem, semibold
- **H2**: 1.5rem, semibold
- **H3**: 1.25rem, medium
- **Body**: 1rem, regular
- **Small**: 0.875rem, regular

## Imagery Guidelines

### Photography Style
If using photos:
- Natural lighting
- Workspace/desk settings
- Focused individuals (not group shots)
- Minimal, uncluttered backgrounds
- Diverse representation

### Illustration Style
If using illustrations:
- Flat, geometric shapes
- Limited color palette (2-3 colors)
- Abstract rather than literal
- Consistent line weights

### Screenshots
- Use realistic but clean data
- Show the value, not complexity
- Highlight one feature at a time
- Use consistent window chrome

---

## Icon Set

Use a consistent icon library:
- **Recommended**: Heroicons (solid or outline)
- **Alternative**: Lucide, Phosphor

### Icon Usage
- Outline for navigation
- Solid for selected/active states
- Consistent sizing (20px for inline, 24px for standalone)

## App Store Assets

### Google Play / App Store Icon
- 512x512 PNG
- Use logo mark only (no text)
- Simple, recognizable at 48px

### Feature Graphic (Google Play)
- 1024x500 PNG
- Show key value proposition
- Logo + tagline + single screenshot

### Screenshots
- Show key flows:
  1. Theme/goal setup
  2. Calendar view with classified events
  3. Weekly planning interface
  4. Daily focus view
  5. AI assistant chat

---

## Writing Guidelines

### Do
- Use active voice
- Be specific about benefits
- Keep sentences short
- Use "you" and "your"

### Don't
- Use jargon without explanation
- Make vague promises
- Use superlatives (best, revolutionary)
- Sound robotic or corporate

### Examples

**Good**: "See where your time goes and whether it connects to your goals."

**Bad**: "Our revolutionary AI-powered productivity platform leverages cutting-edge technology to optimize your workflow."
